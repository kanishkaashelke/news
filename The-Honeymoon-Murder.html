A Tale of Two Narratives

Police & Prosecution's Case

  • A pre-meditated murder conspiracy orchestrated by Sonam and her lover, Raj Kushwaha.
  • The motive was to eliminate Raja to allow Sonam and Raj to be together.
  • Three men were hired as contract killers.
  • Sonam was present at the crime scene and actively participated in the murder and disposal of the body.
  • Substantial evidence including phone chats, CCTV footage, and confessions support the theory.

Accused Families' Defense

  • Sonam is "100% innocent," was abducted, and is being framed by the police.
  • Raj Kushwaha was "framed" and had a purely professional relationship with Sonam.
  • The Meghalaya Police are "misleading the media."
  • The families have vocally demanded a Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) probe.
  • They claim the police narrative is false and their children are innocent.

What's Next?
