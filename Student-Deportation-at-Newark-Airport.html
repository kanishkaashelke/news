A Shifting Landscape

An analysis of the recent surge in US deportations of Indian nationals and the profound impact on student aspirations.

The Flashpoint: An Incident at Newark Airport

A viral video in June 2025 showing an Indian national being forcibly restrained at Newark Airport ignited a firestorm of public concern. While initially thought to be a student, official reports confirmed he had entered the US illegally and was being deported under a court order. The incident, however, became a symbol of a much larger trend.

The Human Impact: "A Pervasive Sense of Fear"
